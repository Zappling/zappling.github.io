About Us

Innovation with Purpose. Zappling was created to deploy engineering solutions in innovative ways that help reduce environmental footprint. With a background in building design, we find new ways to apply our engineering, programming, and business knowledge in the hopes of making buildings – and their occupants – more sustainable. Our team is located in offices around the world, and our headquarters are in lovely Charlottesville, VA.

Zappling, a division of 2rw

Zappling is wholly owned and operated by 2rw Consultants, Inc., an engineering firm dedicated to providing energy conscious and efficient design solutions. Zappling was initially created as the "Web Services" division of 2rw and has since been rebranded. But, we still remain true to our roots - we are dedicated to enabling energy efficiency and conservation.
